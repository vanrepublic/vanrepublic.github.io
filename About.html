Republic of Vanovia

History of Vanovia

The Republic of Vanovia was established on July 6, 2021 by individuals concernd about the spread of Communism, the errosion of the rule of law, and the lack of due respect of vans as a means of transportation.  It is a free and peace-loving state.  

With the adoption of the Constitution of Vanovia in October 2021, Vanovia has successfully functioned as a free and independent state, dedicated to the rule of law, individual rights, and advocating for the return of vans a common form of transportation. 

Vanovia is located in the now-former northeastern United States.  It consists of four main regions: the Vanovian Captial Territory, Gondranland, Auburn Mountain Province, Zenithland, and Dectectivepartment.  

After a brief crisis in December 2021 where the Republic was overthgrown by the self-proclaimed Vanovian Empire, the Republic has been restored and is continuing its mission.
